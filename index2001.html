2001

NO2 levels are not a health concern for citizens of New Jersey, during 2001, but they are quite the problem elsewhere in most of Northeastern America.

NO2 plays a great role in the formation of ozone and acid rain and this is the main reason why it is a major pollutant. While NO2 irritates the lungs and can induce colds, the flu, and bronchitis, ozone affects the lungs, the chest, and even the eyes.
