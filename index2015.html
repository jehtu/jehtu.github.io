2015

September 2015:

The US Environmental Protection Agency accuses Volkswagen of purposely installing software on their vehicles to pass emission tests. It was found that some Volkswagen car emissions can go up to 40 times past the level of Nitrogen oxide emissions permitted.

However, it has also been found that “nearly one-third of on-road heavy-duty diesel vehicle emissions and over half of on-road light-duty diesel vehicle emissions are in excess of certification limits.”

Although the fight against pollution has yielded victories, scientists estimate that nitrogen oxide was involved in 4.4 million premature deaths from air pollution in 2015.
